Module Information

Listening and Speaking Skills

Participate in short oral interactions and present short spoken texts relating to a specified range of familiar and routine event operations contexts involving description, offers, requests and invitations, and expressed in simple language, in order to understand and communicate information and ideas effectively.

Students are expected to be able to, with some assistance:

01

Give information appropriate to purposes

02

Develop interaction through responses

03

04

Use appropriate style and register

05

Use simple grammar & vocabulary

06

Use appropriate non-verbal signals

CA: Briefing

40%

Mode:F2F Briefing
Interlocutors:Staff
Functions:
  • Greetings, introduction, leave-taking
  • Description, Invitation, Thanks
Topics:
  • Event arrangements & Logistics
Duration:
  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Assessment: 2 mins (Video-taped)

Sample (Unit 6)

Section Script Remarks
1. Introduction Good morning. I’m Clair Chow from the Event Management & Operation Section. Today I’m going to brief you on an event order.
2. Background Information Let’s start with the background information. Classy Tailor will be holding a fashion Show at our hotel. It will be on the 18th of February, from 7:30 pm to 10:30 pm at our Grand Ballroom. There will be 130 guests.
3. Duties of Different Teams For the duties of different teams, would the Food and Beverage Team please arrange refreshments, including buffet and drinks stations? For the Front Office Team, please arrange four staff members to greet and guide VIP guests on their arrival. Also, the Marketing Team should coordinate social media activities, and the Event Management & Operations Team will be preparing the welcome packs.
4. Invitation To confirm the event rundown, we will have a site tour with the client at 10 am this Friday. Please join us if possible / if you are free.
5. Closing If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me. Thank you.

Sample (Unit 13)

Role Script
Client:...
Staff: Good morning. This is Rocky Poon from the International Hotel. We’ve received your enquiry about our event packages. May I know more about your event?
Client:...
Staff:

Hint: Describe the details of the package, incl. use of venue, features, complimentary services & pricing

Sure! Our Show & Tell Package would be perfect for your event. The package includes 12 hours' use of our Grand Ballroom. It covers 7,000 square feet of space, and it can accommodate up to 300 guests. The venue features a grand layout, including an 8-metre high ceiling and a 360-degree harbour view. Also, the package includes free-flow soft drinks during the event. The package is priced at $180,000 per event.
Client:...
Staff:

Hint: Describe the add-on items

You can add our dim sum refreshment package for only HK$80 per person. Also, you can book our private meeting rooms for HK$2,000 per day.
Client:...
Staff:

Hint: Describe the conditional offers & offer help

For bookings made 3 months in advance, we’ll offer free parking for up to 5 vehicles on the event day. Student's Self-idea Would you like to book the package now?

Client:...
Staff: No problem. Feel free to call us if you want to make a reservation. Is there anything else I can help you with?
Client:...
Staff: You're most welcome. Have a great day!
